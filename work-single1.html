Design Optimization of a Prybar

This project involved running finite element analysis on a high carbon steel prybar.

Setup

Experimental

In order to figure out what boundary conditions and forces would need to be applied to our model, I designed an experiment that would be conducted on the prybar. weight was added and taken off the curved end of the bar until reaching the point of permenanent deformation.

FEA

The next part was to translate the experimental setup to boundary conditions in SolidWorks.

Results

The highest stress concentration was found to be at the pointed section of the hole used to remove the nail. The only solution we could come up with to combat this stress concentration was to round out that section more.
