Hydraulic Elbow Exoskeleton

For this project I lead a team of seven in designing a wearable structure to house a hydraulic "muscle" and pumping system that would aid the user in lifting objects.

Design

The design consists of a shoulder harness, forearm strap and a pouch that is attached at the user's lower back.The shoulder harness acts as an anchor for the artifical muscle. When the muscle contracts, it pulls on the forearm strap; therefore pulling up the user's arm.
